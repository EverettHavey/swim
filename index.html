Your Laps, Our Rewards.

Turn Your Swim into Earnings

We believe in the power of healthy living and the dedication of swimmers. Our program rewards your commitment to fitness by paying you for every lap you complete. By tracking and verifying your swimming activity, we contribute to a healthier community and gather valuable data for aquatic wellness research. Join Swim & Earn today and make your workout pay off!

How It Works

  • 1. Sign Up: Create your free Swim & Earn account.
  • 2. Track Your Swims: Use our integrated tracking system or connect your compatible fitness device.
  • 3. Record Your Laps: Click the "Record Swimming" button after your swim.
  • 4. Earn Rewards: Get paid for every verified lap you complete.
  • 5. Get Paid: Receive your earnings through your preferred payment method.
